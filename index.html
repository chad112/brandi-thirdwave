About Third Wave Coffee

This coffee shop started with a frustration of coffee not being taken as seriously as it should be. Coffee is a bridge of social experience and incredible taste. The name is representative of a movement from coffee being a commodity to an art, and to a life style. Jimmy is opening up Third Wave Coffee in September of 2015. Here are some things to look forward to;

  • Delicious coffee, done the right way
  • A relaxing atmosphere, with few distractions
  • Smiling faces passionate about quality
  • Education on how to pick the perfect coffee for you

Third Wave Coffee is so excited to bring you freshly roasted, in house, coffee with specific emphasis on each step of the coffee experience to make sure you're getting quality every time. Be sure to be checking this website often to make sure you aren't missing out on any pre-opening special offers!
